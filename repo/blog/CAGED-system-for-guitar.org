* Chords

** The =CAGED= rule
As long as the root has the right position, you can use either of the five =CAGED= patterns
to play chord at any position on the fret you want!

** Usage
- All rows are sorted as =CAGED= order.

- Black: The lowest root.
- Brown: The 1st (root).
- Blue: The 3rd (flat).
- Green: The 5th (flat).
- Orange: The 7th (flat).
- Grey: May be Omitted.

(dim, aug, sus, add) chords are not included currently...
You can infer them though.
