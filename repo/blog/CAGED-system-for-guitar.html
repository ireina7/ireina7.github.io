1.7 Usage

  • All rows are sorted as CAGED order.
  • Black: The lowest root.
  • Brown: The 1st (root).
  • Blue: The 3rd (flat).
  • Green: The 5th (flat).
  • Orange: The 7th (flat).
  • Grey: May be Omitted.
